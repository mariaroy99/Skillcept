About Us
Skillcept is an organization that intends to provide a platform where people can use their skillset to identify
their field of interest and excel in the same. One of our major focusses is to ensure equal participation
of women and the non-binary in all the different types of jobs, be it conventional or unconventional in
order to contribute to the eradication of the discrepancies that exist in the society.
We provide a set of courses to enhance the aspirants to perform well in their area of interest. Different
service providers and job or learning aspirants can register in skillcept, where they can connect with each
other via different job opportunities.
